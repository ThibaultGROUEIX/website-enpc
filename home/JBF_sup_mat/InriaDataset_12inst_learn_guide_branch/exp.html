Comments

  • Edges seem to be well preserved, but it could be a consequence of the high definition of the image and the small kernel.
  • The denoising is bad in high frequencies and texture is not preserved (quite logical since we use only the depth map)
  • The learning curve oscillates even in high frequencies because we chose randomly a channel for denoising and some channel can be harder to denoise than another
